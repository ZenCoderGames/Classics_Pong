Game Design Document for Pong

Gameplay Elements: Level, Paddles, Ball
- Level: an enclosed rectangular box surrounded by walls. Left and Right edges are home bases and should be represented with player colors (Blue/Red)
- Paddles: Wide rectangular box that the player controls
- Ball: A circular object that respects 2d Physics of a ball in real life (except gravity)

Gameplay Logic:
- There are 2 Paddles in front of the Left/Right edges of the game board.
- Paddles reflect the ball when hit
- When the game starts, the Ball starts from the center of the game board and randomly chooses a direction and angle to assign its velocity.
- Paddles can go up and down but stop at the walls (Player 1 - W/S and Player 2 - Up/Down arrow)

End Conditions:
- Each player has 3 lives.
- If the ball reaches either edge, the player whose side it is loses a life.
- Victory: when your opponent loses all Lives.

Game Feel:
- The ball should have a trail
- When the ball is hit, do the follow: hit flash, hit pause, camera shake, vfx and sound fx
- When the ball hits a wall edge, flash the battlefield

UI:
- Use the skill make-classic-game-ui for the HTML layout and javascript for the game logic.
- The top left and top right panels represent the Lives left for each player

Additional Notes:
- Use art in the art folder for respective elements like Walls/Ball etc.
- All configurable elements should be defined in config.js
